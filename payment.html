Why do we need your credit card?
Setting up your account costs nothing. Before you can receive disbursements, we require you to provide a credit card that AUTO MOTORS Pay can charge if your account falls into a negative balance.
Your AUTO MOTORS Pay account may have a negative balance if the amount of fees you owe and refunds you have issued exceeds your sales during a settlement period. In that case, Amazon Pay may charge your credit card on file.
