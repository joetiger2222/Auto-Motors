  About us

Since 2020 in the automotive sector, Auto Motors has one year of experience in the field of
import-export of new cars, importing and exporting In Egypt.

We are making efforts to make B2B cars world-leading with excellent prices and services.

Our goal is to win the trust of our partners and build long-term relationships with them.
We offer competitive prices and reliable. We work to find effective solutions to
the unique needs of our customers.

Our network of suppliers is made up of official dealers so that our cars, even used ones,
are 100% guaranteed.

Our headquarters are only 15 km from Frankfurt, the nerve center of European trade.

In Schöneck we have our administrative and commercial office, while in Bremerhaven
the car depot is where the cars are shipped all over the world.
